PARTNERS

Take a look at my friends, I'm sure you'll find great RPG Maker resources.

My friend Raizen is also a plugin creator and author of the amazing Akea Animated Battle System for RPG Maker MZ (which I also support). He has a vast collection of scripts and plugins for VXAce/MV/MZ.
Comuns Plugins is a group of programmers gathered to make available free plugins for RPG Maker MZ. My Gabe MZ - Legacy Plugins collection is part of the collection of plugins made available in the group. You can also find plugins made by Raizen, Jorge_Pixel, Masked, etc...
Holder is a talented artist who has been making amazing battler resources available to RPG Maker since the days of VXAce. His resources are ideal to be used in combination with the Akea Animated Battle System.
Café Gamer is a Brazilian youtuber, in his channel he brings several content about RPG Maker, from gameplays to tutorials. He also has a wonderful didactic course on RPG Maker, which will teach you everything about the engine from the first steps to the completion of a complex project.
The Toca do Dragão podcast, by my friend Richard, covers various themes of nerd culture, from games, movies, anime, series, etc... It even has a very informative episode about RPG Maker, which I highly recommend to be listened .
